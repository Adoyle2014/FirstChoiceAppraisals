First Choice Appraisal Services, Inc.

Our appraisals include digital photos, location maps, flood maps if necessary, floor sketches and the ability to electronically transmit reports to our clients. With our knowledge of the residential real estate mortgage business, high quality reports, latest technology, and consistently fast turn-around times, you can trust us with your appraisal business.

We specialize in 1-4 family homes, land appraisals, Estates, Divorces, Bankruptcy and other private appraisals, and:

  • We maintain a full Errors and Omissions Insurance Policy.
  • We abide by all Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac guidelines.
  • All of our appraisals are performed in accordance with the requirements of the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP) that were adopted by the Appraisal Standards Board (ASB) of The Appraisal Foundation.
  • Our commitment to quality has made us the appraisers you want on your list and made us the industry leader for residential real estate appraisals in the state.
