About Us

First Choice Appraisal Services, Inc. has been appraising residential Real Estate since 1986. Since this time we have seen all the changes in the industry and continue to keep up with all the vast growth. We stay current on classes, all new data and any new information HUD/FHA, MBREA, the State, Fannie Mae or Freddie Mac provides to the industry. Our goals are to provide the client with the best appraisal product in the most timely manner.

We specialize in 1-4 family homes, land appraisals, Estates, Divorces, Bankruptcy and other private appraisals, and:

  • We maintain full Errors and Omissions Insurance Policy.
  • We abide by all Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac guidelines.
  • All of our appraisals are performed in accordance with the requirements of the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP) that were adopted by the Appraisal Standards Board (ASB) of The Appraisal Foundation.
  • Our commitment to quality has made us the appraisers you want on your list and made us the industry leader for residential real estate appraisals in the state.

Confidence in our professional appraisers guarantee quality and speed to maintain your competitive edge.

  • All Appraisers are FHA approved.
  • All Appraisers are Certified Residential Appraisers to handle transaction amounts over $1 million.
  • Approved by the nation’s largest lenders, community banks, credit unions, AMCs, local brokers, Attorney’s and Accountants.
  • Affiliated with local and national professional appraisal organizations.
  • Subscribe to an extensive list of real estate boards and MLS systems.
  • Borrower contact is made within 24 hours from receipt of order.
  • Appraisal delivery typically within 48 hours from inspection.
  • Reliable and courteous status department with extensive support.
